# 📊 Financial Performance Dashboard — Budget vs Actual Analysis (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive Financial Performance Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze 12 months of simulated business financial data. The dashboard compares actual revenue, expenses, and profit against planned budget targets, enabling performance tracking and data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Track monthly revenue, expenses, and profit for a simulated retail business
- Compare actual financial performance against budgeted targets
- Identify favorable and unfavorable variances across months and quarters
- Support management reporting through visual KPIs and interactive charts

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Features Used
- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables and PivotCharts
- Interactive Slicer (month wise filtering)
- VLOOKUP (budget data mapping)
- INDEX-MATCH (dynamic KPI formulas)
- Excel TABLE references
- VBA Macro (automated PivotTable refresh on file open)
- Accounting number formatting
- Dashboard design and layout

## 📈 Dashboard Sheets
- **Budget Data** — Monthly budget targets for revenue and expenses
- **Income VS Expenses** — Transaction level raw data with calculated columns
- **Monthly Rev vs Exp** — PivotTable and chart comparing monthly revenue and expenses
- **Profit Trend** — Monthly profit trend line chart
- **Variance Analysis** — Budget vs actual variance by month
- **Dashboard** — Interactive summary dashboard with KPIs, charts, slicer, and insights

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Revenue consistently exceeded budget targets across all 12 months
- Expenses ran slightly over budget in most months except July and August
- December recorded highest revenue, profit, and revenue variance of the year
- Q4 emerged as the strongest quarter across all financial metrics
- Average profit margin of 39.63% indicates strong and consistent profitability
- Business shows clear upward growth trajectory from January through December
